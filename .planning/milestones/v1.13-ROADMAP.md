# Milestone v1.13: CAM Measurement Integration Sample

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-04-22
**Phases:** 40-42
**Total Plans:** 6

## Overview

`v1.13` delivered a narrow CAM-flavored measurement integration sample in the browser demo without widening the root runtime/package contract. The milestone focused on three outcomes: compose practical CAM workflow names over shipped exact primitives, present those workflows with sample-first wording for workpiece/tool scenarios, and lock docs/governance so CAM semantics remain demo-owned and secondary-surface verification stays conditional.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- Keep CAM workflow naming (`clearance / step depth`, `center-to-center`, `surface-to-center`) demo-owned over shipped exact primitives unless a reusable root/package gap is proven.
- Keep the browser demo sample-first and one-result-oriented instead of reintroducing inspection-product flows.
- Keep `test:secondary:contracts` and demo browser verification conditional, outside `test:release:root`.

**Issues Resolved:**
- Added practical CAM workflow composition and representative workpiece/tool flows without widening runtime/package APIs.
- Tightened sample-first CAM wording and shell framing while preserving deterministic measurement invalidation behavior.
- Realigned root/package/sdk docs and governance contracts to lock CAM ownership boundaries and conditional verification routing.

**Issues Deferred:**
- `SEED-001-web-exact-brep-measurement` remained dormant and was explicitly acknowledged at closeout.
- Broader CAM analytics, probing/tolerance/report workflows, and PMI/CMM product behavior remain outside current repo scope.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**
- `v1.13` closed without a dedicated milestone-audit artifact; closeout relied on phase evidence, contract suites, browser verification, and planning audit.
