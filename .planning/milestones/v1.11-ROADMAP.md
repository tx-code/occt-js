# Milestone v1.11: Supported Semantic Helpers

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-04-21
**Phases:** 36
**Total Plans:** 1

## Overview

`v1.11` closed as a deliberately narrowed milestone. The shipped result is package-first compound-hole helper support over the existing exact measurement surface: `counterbore` and `countersink` now ship through `@tx-code/occt-core` with one narrow additive carrier descriptor. The broader candidate-analysis, demo candidate suggestion, pinned/exportable session workflow, and productivity-governance follow-on were dropped before acceptance and are not part of the shipped milestone outcome.
